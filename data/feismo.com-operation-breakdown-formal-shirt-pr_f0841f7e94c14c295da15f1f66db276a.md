Operation Breakdown :

• Attach pocket to front by setting pocket and stitching
• Attach yoke to back
• Attach front to the back
• Make collar – collar + band
• Attach collar to body
• Make sleeves – cuffs + sleeves
• Attach sleeves to body
• Side seam attachment
• Button hole making and stitching
• Washing and drying
• Button attachment
• Label attachments
• Finishing Operations

Bottle Necks

• Collar making and attachment is one of the critical operation, it takes more time due to

interlining and number of stitches involved in it.

• Thread – if good quality of thread is not used than it will lead to more breakdown.
• Needle – if high quality of stainless steel is not used than it will lead to more breakdowns.
• Button hole and button stitching takes more time.
• Moving of semi-finished shirt from one sewing machine to another, from one operation

to another takes more time.

• Worker being flooded with overload of work would not able to complete the operation.
•

Improper tensions leads to puckering.

Suggestion

• Thread to be used should be 2 ply yarns with good strength.
• The needle used should be high strength steel.
• Tensioning device should be maintained and oiled properly.
• The temperature during should be maintained properly according to fabric.
• Fabric fastness properties should be considered before washing.
• Button should be attached after washing in order to make washing easy.
